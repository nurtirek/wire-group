06 Onboarding

Three steps. Under two minutes.

No installs, no app store, no password to remember. iikoAnalytics lives inside Telegram.

  1. 01

    Server URL

    Paste your iiko server URL (the one your iikoOffice uses).

  2. 02

    Login

    Your existing iiko username. No new accounts to create.

  3. 03

    Password → branches

    Enter password, pick the branches you want to see. Done.

Security · the details

  • Branch isolation lives in iiko itself — a manager sees only their location, an owner sees the network. No manual role-mapping.
  • Full logout wipes credentials from the database and the device.
  • Mini App auth via Telegram initData (HMAC signature). No separate passwords.
  • Backend in FastAPI, persistent on Render, integration with iiko Server REST 9.x.

Built for

  • ◈ Restaurant chain owners (2–20 locations)
  • ◈ Managers running a single venue
  • ◈ Accountants and buyers who post invoices

Why Telegram, not an app
